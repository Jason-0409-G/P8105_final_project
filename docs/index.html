About This Project

Our Mission

Data Science I Final Project

This project analyzes trends in dental caries prevalence and preventive dental care utilization across diverse populations. Using national and California real-world datasets, we aim to inform public health interventions and improve oral health outcomes.

Our comprehensive analysis combines statistical modeling, data visualization, and epidemiological methods to uncover patterns and insights that can guide policy decisions and clinical practice.
